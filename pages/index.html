Who are we?

Aquacycle is an innovative start-up dedicated to sustainability and technology in the agricultural sector. With a mission to transform the improper disposal of batteries into an ecological solution, the company develops low-cost irrigation sensors from reused components. These smart sensors monitor soil moisture in real time, allowing small and medium-sized farmers to irrigate more efficiently, reducing water waste and increasing crop yields. By combining technological innovation and environmental responsibility, Aquacycle is positioning itself as a leader in sustainable agricultural practices.

Sustainability and Innovation in the Field

At Aquacycle, we believe that innovation can go hand in hand with sustainability. Our mission is to turn an environmental problem into an affordable solution for small and medium-sized farmers. We collect used batteries and, through an innovative process, reuse their components to develop low-cost irrigation sensors.


These smart sensors monitor soil moisture in real time, enabling precise and efficient irrigation, saving water and increasing agricultural productivity. With our solution, farmers can reduce the waste of resources and optimize their crops, while contributing to a more sustainable planet by helping to solve the problem of improper battery disposal.


At Aquacycle, we are cultivating a greener future with affordable and sustainable technology. Join us on this journey of innovation and environmental responsibility.

Innovating with Sustainability for a Better Future

At Aquacycle, we believe that the future depends on the choices we make today. Our commitment to sustainability goes beyond offering efficient technological solutions; we are creating a positive impact for generations to come. By turning used batteries into affordable irrigation systems, we reduce waste and prevent harmful materials from ending up in landfills.


We know that preserving natural resources is essential to ensuring a healthy planet for our children and grandchildren. That's why our solutions are designed to help farmers save water and improve the efficiency of their crops, promoting more sustainable and regenerative practices in the field.


Every innovation we develop at Aquacycle is designed with the future in mind. We want to leave a legacy of environmental responsibility, offering affordable and sustainable technology so that future generations can thrive in a more balanced and conscious world.

Turning Waste into Opportunities for Sustainable Agriculture

Aquacycle is an innovative startup that collects used batteries to turn them into low-cost irrigators, benefiting small farmers and underprivileged communities. With a focus on sustainability, our proposal not only reduces waste, but also offers efficient solutions for water use, promoting more sustainable agriculture.


As well as addressing environmental issues, Aquacycle is committed to encouraging food diversity. Our affordable irrigators make it possible to grow a variety of foods, such as fruit, vegetables and legumes, which are essential for a balanced and nutritious diet. We believe that by facilitating access to irrigation, we are contributing to the food security of communities.


In addition, our initiative promotes the production of plant proteins, such as beans and grains, which are essential for health and nutrition. With Aquacycle, each irrigator represents an opportunity for development and a step towards agricultural practices that respect biodiversity, promoting healthier food for all.
